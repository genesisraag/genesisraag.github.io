Any tips for breaking through a weight loss plateau?

new_user52:

I've hit a weight loss plateau, and it's frustrating. Despite my efforts, the scale won't budge. Any tips or advice on how to break through this plateau and continue making progress towards my weight loss goals?

experienced_user23:

Weight loss plateaus are a common challenge, but you can overcome them with some strategic adjustments. Firstly, consider reassessing your calorie intake your body may have adapted to your current level. Mix up your workout routine by incorporating high-intensity interval training (HIIT) or trying new forms of exercise to shock your system. Ensure you're getting enough sleep, as lack of sleep can impact weight loss. Lastly, stay patient and persistent plateaus happen, but with the right adjustments, you can push through. Share your experiences and get additional tips from Fit Vibes community members who have successfully navigated similar plateaus. You're not alone in this journey!
