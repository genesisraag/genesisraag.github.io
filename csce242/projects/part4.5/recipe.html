Quinoa Salad with Roasted Vegetables and Lemon-Tahini Dressing

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups mixed vegetables (such as cherry tomatoes, bell peppers, zucchini, and red onion), chopped
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup chickpeas, cooked (canned, drained, and rinsed)
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped

For the Lemon-Tahini Dressing:

  • 3 tablespoons tahini
  • 2 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon honey or maple syrup
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Water (to adjust consistency)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the chopped vegetables on a baking sheet. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and toss to coat. Roast in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes or until vegetables are tender and slightly golden.
  3. While the vegetables are roasting, cook the quinoa according to package instructions. Once cooked, fluff it with a fork and let it cool slightly.
  4. In a small bowl, whisk together tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, honey or maple syrup, salt, and pepper. Add water gradually to achieve your desired dressing consistency.
  5. In a large bowl, combine the cooked quinoa, roasted vegetables, chickpeas, and fresh parsley. Pour the Lemon-Tahini Dressing over the mixture and toss gently to coat.
  6. Divide the quinoa salad into bowls and garnish with additional parsley if desired. Serve it warm or at room temperature.
