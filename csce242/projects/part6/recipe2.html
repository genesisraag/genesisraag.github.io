Baked Salmon with Lemon and Dill

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 lemon, thinly sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Lemon wedges for serving

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  3. Drizzle olive oil over the fillets and season with salt and pepper.
  4. Sprinkle minced garlic evenly over the salmon fillets.
  5. Place lemon slices on top of each fillet.
  6. Sprinkle fresh dill over the top of each fillet.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork. Cooking time may vary based on the thickness of the fillets.
  8. Carefully transfer the baked salmon to serving plates.
  9. Garnish with additional fresh dill.
  10. Serve with lemon wedges on the side.
